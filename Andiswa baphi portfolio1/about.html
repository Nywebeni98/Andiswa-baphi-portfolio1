About me

Mafaku, a rising DJ, has finely honed her skills over the past 4 years, gracing venues like Sportscene DJ series, Grand West Casino, Gin & Tonic events, Zeits Mocca, Time Out Market, and Cabo Beach Club. Since 2022, she has been a prominent presence at silent events across various locations and made a noteworthy appearance at Bazique Festival in 2023.

Mafaku holds the position of resident DJ at Tiger's Milk Claremont, with additional bookings from affiliated establishments such as La Parada (Kloof Street and Century City) and Tiger's Milk (Kloof Street, Century City, Long Street, and Camps Bay). Fueled by a love for Deep House, Afro House, Afro Tech, and classic house, Mafaku finds inspiration in DJ Zinhle, Nastee Nev, Black Motion, Shimza, the Godfathers of Deephouse, and some artists under the Stay True Sounds record label. With a devoted fan base, she aspires to continue sharing stages with established artists and craft her own musical creations in the near future.
